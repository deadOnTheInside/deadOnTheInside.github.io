Asking Alexandria - The Black (March 25, 2016).

By far the best albums this band has ever released, mainly due to their new singer, Denis Shaforostov (formerly Down & Dirty, Make Me Famous) and his melodic singing voice as well as crushing gutturals and eardrum-tearing highs.

Overall this album was amazing, every song was great. A few songs could have been done better but the perfect singles released easily more than make up for that.

The guitar in these songs is amazing as well, showing off their wonderful guitarists’ skills.

All the way from a steel guitar in I Won’t Give In to bone-crushing breakdowns in Let It Sleep.

The lyrics, co-written by Denis and the guitarist Ben Bruce, were beautiful on every last track, ranging from stadium anthems to hatred-filled fist-pumpers and lighter-waving ballads.

9.1/10

Favourite track: I Won't Give In

The Amity Affliction - Chasing Ghosts (September 7, 2015).

The most amazing of all four of the band’s albums, although not by much.

The steady guitar rhythms interspersed with breakdowns, the clean singer/bass player’s, Ahren Stringer, amazing voice, and Joel Birch’s growls make this an album that every last metalcore fan should hear and love.

I would advise any listeners to listen once to each song in a row through the whole album and then listen to each while scrolling through the lyrics, probably a few times for each song.

You can never get tired of these absolute masterpieces so by all means have it on repeat for the next few weeks.

9.9(because nothing’s perfect)/10

Favourite track: Open Letter
